# AsyncSkills Marketing Plan

## Overview
AsyncSkills is a skills-first job board designed for remote and asynchronous work, focusing on demonstrable abilities rather than traditional interviews. This marketing plan outlines strategies for launching in Atlanta and scaling nationally.

## Mission & Value Proposition

**Mission**: Revolutionize hiring by connecting skilled professionals with companies through portfolio-based matching, eliminating traditional "tell me about yourself" interviews.

**Value Proposition**:
- **Skills-First Hiring**: Showcase actual work instead of resumes
- **Remote-First Culture**: Designed for distributed teams
- **Inclusive by Design**: Universal benefits for all users
- **Atlanta Ecosystem**: Deep local market knowledge and connections

## Target Audience

### Primary Audience
- **Job Seekers**: Skilled professionals who struggle with traditional interviews
- **Neurodivergent Talent**: Indirect support through skills-first approach
- **Remote Workers**: Professionals seeking async work environments
- **Career Changers**: Portfolio-driven candidates

### Secondary Audience
- **Companies**: Tech startups, remote-first organizations, inclusive employers
- **Recruiters**: Those seeking diverse, skilled talent pools
- **Atlanta Tech Community**: Local ecosystem participants

## Competitive Positioning

### Unique Value Proposition
1. **Skills-First Approach**: Portfolio over resume, demonstrable abilities
2. **Remote/Async Focus**: Designed for distributed work cultures
3. **Atlanta Expertise**: Deep local market knowledge and relationships
4. **Inclusive Design**: Universal accessibility benefits all users
5. **Community-Driven**: Open source with strong community support

### Competitive Advantages
- **Local Atlanta Focus**: Understanding of tech ecosystem and companies
- **Skills Matching Algorithm**: AI-powered portfolio analysis
- **Community Network**: Atlanta tech community connections
- **Inclusive Approach**: Supports diversity initiatives without discrimination
- **Open Source**: Community trust and contributions

### Competitor Analysis

| Platform | Focus | Pricing | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|----------|-------|---------|-----------|------------|
| **LinkedIn** | Professional Network | Premium subscriptions | Network effects | Traditional hiring bias |
| **Indeed** | General Jobs | CPC/PPA | Job volume | Generic matching |
| **We Work Remotely** | Remote Jobs | $299/post | Quality curation | Expensive posting |
| **FlexJobs** | Remote Jobs | $49-499/month | Hand-screened | Limited job volume |
| **AsyncSkills** | Skills-First Remote | $39-299/month | Portfolio matching | New platform |
